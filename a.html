ROUND 1

Round 1 comprises of 4 domains from which participants
have to select at least 2 domains and can choose maximum
of 4 domains for more points.The more domain you opt for,
the more you score.

The four domains are:

Vintage Photography | Leaf Collage | Origami Art | Face Mask Art

ROUND 2

Test your knowledge just by answering some basic questions
and gain points easily Round 2 contains quiz questions based
on popular TV series and sports.

The field are as follows:

Football | Strange Things | Cricket | Friends

ROUND 3

The final round consists of making an interesting and astonishing
magic trick video which might force people to say
"How did you do that!".The best and the most surprising video
will be selected as the winner.
